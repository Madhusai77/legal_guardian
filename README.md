Millions of users fall into **predatory lending traps**:

* ⚠️ 10–30% monthly interest rates
* ⚠️ Hidden charges & unfair clauses
* ⚠️ Continuous harassment & threats
* ⚠️ Lack of legal awareness

👉 Result: Users panic and make **financially harmful decisions**

---

## ❗ Core Gap

> There is no system that helps users **test decisions before acting** in high-pressure financial situations.

---

# 💡 Solution — DebtTrapEnv

> A simulation-based AI platform that helps users **analyze, simulate, and choose the safest financial actions**

---

# ⚙️ How It Works

```text
User Input
   ↓
AI Analysis (RBI rules + risk detection)
   ↓
Simulation Engine (lender reacts)
   ↓
Strategy Optimization
   ↓
Final Action Plan
```

```text

        ↓

        ↓
DebtTrapEnv Engine
   ├── State (Borrower Profile)
   ├── Actions (Decision Space)

Score + Strategy Output
```
